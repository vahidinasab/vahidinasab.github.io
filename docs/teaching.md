# Teaching

## TEACHING INTERESTS
In addition to my sound research background, I have solid teaching experience as well. I have pursued every available opportunity to teach. During my graduate and postgraduate training, I was also fortunate enough to have served as a Thesis Supervisor/Co-Supervisor for 11 PhD, 40 MSc and 26 BSc students in the Electric Power Engineering.

Beyond these experiences, I am deeply committed to undergraduate teaching and postgraduate supervision. My teaching roles have included instructor, lecturer and mentor. I found that my research experience provided me with a broad view that is useful in assisting students with projects.
